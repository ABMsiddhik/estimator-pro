FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.


How does the WhatsApp billing integration work?
Once you generate an estimate, click the "Send on WhatsApp" button. The system pre-fills a formatted message with all material details, quantities, GST, and total — and opens WhatsApp with the customer's number pre-filled. You just tap Send. No manual copying needed.
Does it support GST invoice generation for multiple tax rates?
Yes! EstimatePro supports all GST slabs (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) with automatic CGST/SGST/IGST split. You can set different GST rates per product category, and the system handles the calculation automatically.
Can I add my own product catalog and pricing?
Absolutely. While we provide a pre-loaded catalog of popular brands and materials, you can fully customize product names, codes, rates, and units. You can also update prices anytime from the admin panel.
Is the platform mobile-friendly for field use?
Yes! EstimatePro is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones. Your sales team can create and send estimates directly from a customer's site without needing a laptop.
Can I use my own business branding on estimates?
Business and Enterprise plans include full custom branding — upload your logo, set your business name, address, GSTIN, bank details, and custom terms. Estimates will look exactly like your professional letterhead.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on our Business plan — no credit card required. You'll have access to all features so you can experience the full value before committing.
